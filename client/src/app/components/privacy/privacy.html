Privacy Policy

Statement of Commitment

At Jewellify, we honor the trust you place in us when sharing your personal information. Our privacy policy is designed to provide transparency regarding the data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it. This commitment is central to our reputation as a trusted luxury jeweler.

Information We Collect

Personal Identification

Includes your name, email address, physical address, and telephone number provided during account creation or checkout.

Transaction Records

Details of your purchases, payment history (excluding full credit card numbers), and interaction with our client services.

Digital Footprint

IP addresses, browser types, and navigation patterns collected via cookies to enhance your personalized shopping experience.

Preferences

Information regarding your wishlist items, preferred categories, and communication choices.

How We Use Your Data

  • To facilitate seamless order fulfillment and logistics.
  • To provide tailored product recommendations based on your unique style.
  • To ensure the security of your account and prevent fraudulent transactions.
  • To communicate exclusive previews and news (with your prior consent).
  • To improve our digital platform through advanced data analytics.

Your Rights

You maintain full control over your personal information. You have the right to access, rectify, or request the deletion of your data at any time. For any privacy-related inquiries, please contact our dedicated data protection team at privacy@jewellify.com.
