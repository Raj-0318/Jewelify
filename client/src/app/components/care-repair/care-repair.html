Jewelry Care Guidelines

Preserve the luminosity of your Jewellify pieces by following these simple guidelines. Fine jewelry is delicate and should be handled with utmost care.

  • Remove jewelry before sleeping, bathing, or swimming.
  • Avoid direct contact with perfumes, lotions, and harsh chemicals.
  • Store your pieces individually in the provided soft Jewellify pouches to prevent scratching.
  • Clean gently with a soft, dry, lint-free cloth.
