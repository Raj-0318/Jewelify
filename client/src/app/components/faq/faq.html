Frequently Asked Questions

How do I determine my ring size?

We offer a complimentary Ring Sizing Guide that you can download and print from our website. Alternatively, you can visit any Jewellify boutique for a professional measurement, or request a physical sizer to be mailed to your residence.

Can I customize an existing design?

Absolutely. Many of our pieces can be personalized with engravings or alternative gemstone selections. For more significant modifications, we invite you to explore our Custom Jewelry service to create a truly bespoke creation.

Do you provide ethically sourced gemstones?

Ethical sourcing is the cornerstone of Jewellify. All our diamonds are conflict-free and sourced in accordance with the Kimberley Process. We prioritize suppliers who adhere to the highest social and environmental standards.

What is your return policy for gifts?

Gifts purchased at Jewellify can be returned or exchanged within 30 days of delivery, provided they are in their original, pristine condition. The recipient may exchange the item for a piece of equal value or receive a Jewellify credit.

How should I store my jewelry?

To maintain the brilliance of your pieces, we recommend storing them individually in the soft Jewellify pouches provided. Keep them in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and avoid contact with other jewelry to prevent surface abrasions.
