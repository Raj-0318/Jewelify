## Slide 20: Database – Products Collection
* **name (String):** Product name & brand
* **category (String):** Product category
* **price / discount (Number):** Pricing details
* **stock (Number):** Real-time inventory
* **images (Array):** Product photo URLs
* **variations (Object):** Size, clarity, material

---

## Slide 21: Database – Users Collection
* **name / email (String):** User identity fields
* **password (String):** Secures user account
* **role (String):** Admin or Customer
* **wishlist (Array):** Saved product IDs
* **addresses (Array):** Multiple shipping addresses

---

## Slide 22: Database – Orders Collection
* **userId (ObjectId):** Links order to user
* **products (Array):** Items, quantity & price
* **status (String):** Order lifecycle stage
* **shippingInfo (Object):** Delivery address details
* **createdAt (Date):** Timestamp of order
